The program also checked for invalid values such as negative savings or invalid payment timing.

Two possible problems were that some parts of the formula were difficult to understand at first and the code still needed to be tested carefully. Another issue is that AI-generated code may not always perfectly match course style guidelines or assignment expectations. It also looked very odd and didnt really look formatted correctly what so ever.
